# Research: Custom Branded Water Bottle Business - Pakistan Market

## Market Overview
- Pakistan bottled water market: ~$1.2B annually, growing at 8-10% CAGR
- Major cities: Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad
- B2B segment (restaurants, hotels, corporate, events) is underserved for custom branding
- Key competitors: Nestle Pure Life, Aquafina, Kinley (none offer easy custom branding)

## Target Customer Segments
1. **Restaurants & Cafes** - Want their own branded water instead of selling other brands
2. **Wedding & Event Planners** - Custom labeled water for walimas, mehndis, corporate events
3. **Corporate Offices** - Branded water for meetings, conferences, reception areas
4. **Hotels & Hospitality** - Premium branded water experience for guests
5. **Healthcare** - Hospitals, clinics wanting professional branded water

## Key Value Propositions
- "Every sip promotes your brand"
- Replace branded water (Nestle/Aquafina) with YOUR own brand
- Premium feel for guests/customers
- Cost-effective marketing tool
- Eco-friendly options available
- Low minimum order quantities

## Pakistan-Specific Considerations
- Mobile-first market (70%+ traffic from mobile)
- WhatsApp is primary business communication channel
- Cash on delivery preferred
- Urdu + English bilingual audience
- Social proof (testimonials, portfolio) very important
